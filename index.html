UTTAR BANGA KRISHI VISHWAVIDYALAYA

UBKV, also known as Uttar Banga Krishi Viswavidyalaya, is a state university located in Cooch Behar, West Bengal, India. The university was established in 2001 with a mission to provide education, research, and extension activities in the field of agriculture, horticulture, and allied sciences. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines such as agriculture, horticulture, forestry, fisheries, and agricultural engineering.

There are 12 teams consisting of students from UG, PG, and PhD programs.

UBKV PREMIER LEAGUE

The UPL, also known as UBKV Premier League, is one of the biggest cricket tournaments in Cooch Behar and is highly demanded by the students of UBKV. This cricket tournament is known for its fast-paced and exciting gameplay, as the matches are played in a short-hand format with short-pitched deliveries, making it an edge-of-the-seat experience for the spectators. One unique feature of the UPL is its auction-based system, where each team is allotted 500 points to select their players from a pool of registered players. This system ensures a fair and competitive playing field for all the teams and makes the tournament even more exciting. The UPL has become a popular event among the students and faculty members of UBKV, and the tournament is known for its electrifying atmosphere and enthusiastic supporters. It provides a platform for young and talented cricketers to showcase their skills and compete with their peers in a friendly and competitive environment. Overall, the UBKV Premier League is a great initiative by UBKV to promote sportsmanship, teamwork, and healthy competition among its students and staff, while also providing a fun and entertaining experience for cricket enthusiasts in Cooch Behar.
